Analysis of Sightings with Census Data

Description

Shows the number of sightings with various (population, literacy) combinations. Hovering on one of the areas shows how many people were in that classification for a given population and literacy. Visualization can be modified in many ways like sorting by size or alphabetically. Classes can also be dragged around to view in any way required
