Cancer Incidences in different Races



States

Graph Details


The above graph plots cancer incidence counts in whites, hispanics and all races (cummulative) for each year across the different states in the United States of America. Using the select box, we can filter the different races to observe the distributions. The States legend to the left of the graph maps the states (in codes) to their colors on the maps. Clicking on the color boxes will filter the state information.Using the clear all or show all button, incidences in different states can be filtered.

Inferences


Hispanics

California, Texas, Florida and New York face the highest instances of cancer in places where UFO's have been sighted.
Mississippi has the fewest instances of cancer.

Whites

California, Florida, New York, Texas, and Pennsylvania following closely, face the highest instances of cancer in places where UFO's have been sighted.
Hawaii and Wyoming has the fewest instances of cancer.

All Races

Cummulatively, we observe that California has the most number of cancer incidences reported, followed by Texas and Florida closely. New York and Pennsylvania too face the very high instances of cancer in places where UFO's have been sighted.
Overall Wyoming has the fewest instances of cancer in all races as observed frok UFO sightings in these areas.
